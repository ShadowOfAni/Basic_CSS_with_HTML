jis university

JIS University, established in 2014, is a private university in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. It aims to redefine higher education in India by focusing on industry-focused education, research, and skill development. JIS University is recognized by various bodies like UGC, AICTE, and AIU, and has received several awards for its contributions to education.
